Sculpture & Craft
Lights of Passage

Lights of Passage — Pointe shoes & mirrored tiles. I’ve lost count of the nights I’ve spent under a mirror ball—seeking solace, slipping into reverie, or finding myself there by chance. The dance floor has always meant something to me—be it the jewel-box-like ballet studio or the abyss of a nightclub. In this piece, I wanted to bring together my two worlds of dance, taking a pair of my rigid pointe shoes and transforming them into lightness through the application of mirrored tiles. The shoes, once symbols of discipline and endurance, become something else entirely—icons not to be suffered in, but to be worshipped under. They become a beacon of self-expression, guiding movement, music, and the endless moments of letting go. #thislifeislikeadancefloor

A boundless reservoir
Seattle · Available · Open

A boundless reservoir of all things poetic. I work at the intersection of research, strategy, and service design — planning and executing the work that uncovers user needs, pain points, and opportunities, then translating those findings into journeys, frameworks, and experiences that deliver on business goals. Insight and design as one discipline, not two.

I believe the best ideas live at the edge of disciplines — that a body in space knows things a spreadsheet never will, and that the most enduring brands are built on a kind of poetic logic: rigorous, felt, and impossible to copy.
